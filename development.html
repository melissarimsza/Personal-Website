Edges2Kirby

Edges2Kirby is a machine learning based web project. It uses a custom-made model applied with tensorflow.js to color fill a user drawn kirby.

Try it yourself!

2048

2048 is a desktop number-based puzzle game. Combine numbers on the game board to make matches and work your way up through powers of two. Win the game by combinging enough numbers to reach 2048!

Prime Power

Prime Power is an iOS number-based puzzle game. Combine numbers on the game board to make matches. Win the game by combinging enough numbers to reach the power of 11!

Sun Devil Fitness Center Wordle

Based on the sensation Wordle (owned by the New York Times), SDFC Wordle is a custom version created for the Arizona State Sun Devil Fitness Center. It features health and wellness based words and ASU branding.

Try it yourself!

Hotdog/Not Hotdog

Based off of the app "Seefood" from the TV show Silicon Valley. This goofy app combines the power of the Apple MobileNetV2 image classifier with the ease and functionality of SwiftUI to create a hotdog identifying app.
